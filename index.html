Vaibhav Gupta (Vaib)

Vaibhav is a dynamic entrepreneur and esteemed academic. Earning his Ph.D. from the University of Australia in 2023, his groundbreaking research delved into the legitimacy challenges faced by successors in Australian family businesses, uncovering diverse strategies for gaining legitimacy. Since 2017, he has been imparting his expertise in Strategy and Entrepreneurship at the University of South Australia. Before his academic tenure, Vaibhav's 16-year entrepreneurial journey was marked by the successful creation, establishment, and exit of multiple businesses. His exceptional ability to rapidly acquire new knowledge and devise innovative solutions is a testament to his strengths. In addition to his research and teaching endeavors, Vaibhav is actively developing a startup.

He is currently expanding his skill set by learning to code websites and mobile apps, showcasing his relentless pursuit of growth and innovation.
